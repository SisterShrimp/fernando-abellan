Biography

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) in Trondheim, working under the supervision of Rune Haugseng. Previously, I completed my PhD at the University of Hamburg, where I was supervised by Tobias Dyckerhoff. My research interests lie in higher category theory, with a focus on its applications to homotopy theory and geometry. Recently, I have developed a growing interest in synthetic approaches to higher category theory, particularly through the framework of (∞,2)-topoi.
